I teach broadly in Computer Science; my quite varied background is in Security, Reinforcement Learning algorithms, chem-informatics, and Digital Signal Processing (video codecs).

ahmed [[dot]] hamza \at/ colorado \dot\ edu

My interests sit at the intersection of AI and Computing Security. I try to answer questions like "How can this model misbehave, and what does it look like when it does?" as well as more general questions of capability — "can a system learn to act in a useful way in adversarial autonomous settings?". My teaching is heavily within systems programming and adversarial cybersecurity + reverse engineering.
